CAREER COUNSELLING SCHEMES

Career Counselling Schemes helps the students to understand the career opportunities and optionsavailable. This type of training helps them to analyze their strengths and weaknesses and helps in finding the perfect career path in the future. Guru Nanak Institute of Technology organizes career counseling programs every year for the benefit of students from 2 nd to 4 th year through the year.

Some of the major schemes include:
1.Technical Training based on Aptitude and verbal and non-verbal reasoning
2.Mock test sessions
3.Mock Tests based on the test packages of the external vendors
4.Pre-placement talks
5.Mock interview sessions
Counselors and Experts from the institute and also external worlds are invited for the training sessions for the benefit of the students. The ultimate aim of these schemes are to develop the technical and soft skills of the students, so that they can crack the placement drives successfully.

PREPARATION FOR COMPETITIVE EXAMINATIONS

Preparation for Competitive exams will enhance the skill of understanding the application of concepts, which is required when students appear exams like GATE, GRE, IELTS. Guru Nanak Institute of Technology used to arrange competitive examinations every year for the benefit of students of 3 rd and 4 th year throughout the semester. It will help students in logical and analytical thinking and in building a strong foundation for a career. The classes for GATE exams are conducted from 2 nd year to final year from department as well as institute level. Special sessions are conducted by the counselors and experts from the field for motivating the students for appearing the competitive examinations.
