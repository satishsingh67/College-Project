GURU NANAK INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

HOSTEL FACILITIES

In every institution there are some students who stay far away from the institution , they have to stay at rented houses or mess which completely lags the home atmosphere. in order to make these students feel comfortable and provide an home like atmosphere the institute also provide it's students the facility of hostels. There are seperated hostels for boys as well as girls each looked and cared by the in charge wardens and there are several features that the students preferring to stay at hostels will get that are covered below. for availing the hostel facility a student can apply during their admission in the institute and pay the hostel fees semi annually. The hostel charges are covered in details in the fees structure itself.

Facilities

Food Service/ Food Runner

almost each hostel has a mess of its own, run by the university administration together with students' participation. There is a student Mess Secretary post, for which every month a student is elected and whose job is to decide the menu and maintain the quality of food in such a way that the expenses (per day) do not go beyond a critical value. The usual 'basic' per day for 3 meals (breakfast, lunch & dinner). That said, the menu for all the 3 meals depends on the day of the week. For lunch and dinner, Dal and Sambar or Rasam are served on all days of the week, with the curry or fry varying based on the day of the week.

Refreshment

We had the large area for hostels with a playground, gym, and indoor games also. The first year students can have a separate block for hostel where the other are having another blocks. In a room has 3 members and attached bathroom.

Housekeeping

Rooms were good and they used to get a regular cleaning. At day times the food in Hostel was good.
